Who am I?
I'm just a number, literally. I can't tell you that number because I would be force choked until my head popped like a balloon. Believe me, I've seen it happen (RIP Bob). I'm just your average everyday Buckethead who puts his armor on one leg at a time. I'm currently stationed on Death Star 2. Don't ask me about Death Star 1 (what a mess that was), "official" word is that it never happened so we will leave it at that.

I was born on a mid rim planet to a poor family of moisture farmers. At the age of 6 I was sold to the Empire for trooper training and have been with the trooper corp ever since. I've been trained in hand to hand combat, a little on blasters, and hope to start training with electrostaff's soon!

My future goals are to find those pesky droids, just kidding, I know they're scrap heaps by now. I want to complete construction of my Sith saber and hitch a ride to the Outer Rim. Once there I will track down some Sith and beg to be trained in the ways of the dark side.
